# Marka — Marketing Inteligente para PMEs Locais

Marka is a marketing strategy service for small and medium-sized local businesses (PMEs), built around AI-generated, sector-specific marketing plans delivered in 48 hours at a fraction of traditional agency costs.

## Sites

### `marketingpme-site.html`
Main marketing site targeting both restauração (restaurants, cafés, catering) and construção civil (builders, renovators, tradespeople). Includes hero, niche sections, how-it-works, pricing (€97 / €197 / €397/month), testimonials, and intake form.

### `marketingobra-site.html`
Hyper-niche site focused exclusively on construção civil. Covers 6 specialisations: empreiteiro geral, remodelações, carpintaria, instalações elétricas, canalização, and pavimentos. Includes a detailed intake form with construction-specific fields.

### `oferta-gratuita.html`
Conversion-focused landing page for the temporary free plan offer. Includes a live countdown timer, vacancy counter, value comparison, and a sign-up form with a mandatory testimonial agreement checkbox.

### `marka-identidade-visual.html`
Complete brand identity guide: logo (SVG, all variants), icon construction rationale, colour palette (5 colours), typography system (Fraunces + Outfit), usage rules, and real-world mockups (business card, email signature, social post, favicon).

## Service Delivery Process

1. Client fills intake form (~10 min)
2. Sector, market, and competitor analysis via AI tools
3. Personalised marketing plan generated and refined
4. Delivery as PDF or Notion document
5. 30-min presentation call
6. Monthly updates (recurring plans)

---

## Go-to-Market

The initial acquisition strategy uses a **free plan in exchange for a testimonial**, targeting 5 local PMEs per week across WhatsApp, LinkedIn, Instagram, and cold email. Full outreach scripts are available in the project documentation.

## Getting Started

1. Clone or download the repository
2. Open any `.html` file directly in a browser — no server required
3. To publish: upload files to your static host of choice
4. Register `marka-portugal.pt` and point DNS to your host
5. Replace placeholder text (`[teu nome]`, `[email]`, `[telemóvel]`) with real contact details
